# Decision 016: In-App Communication

**Status:** Decided

**Category:** Collaboration Model

**Question:** How will users communicate within the app — through comments, mentions, notifications, or other mechanisms?

**Decision:** Comments, @mentions, and in-app notifications. Users can discuss work via comments on tasks, direct attention to specific people with @mentions, and receive in-app notifications for relevant activity.

**Rationale:** This provides the complete communication loop a medium team expects. The activity feed from Decision 010 covers general awareness, while in-app notifications serve personal, actionable alerts (mentions, assignments, etc.). Email notifications can be added later without rearchitecting.
