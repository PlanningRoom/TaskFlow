# Decision 001: Primary Audience

**Status:** Decided

**Category:** Target Users & Market

**Question:** Who is the primary audience for TaskFlow — is it for internal team use only, or will it also serve external customers?

**Decision:** Invite-only access. Users can only join TaskFlow when invited by an existing member with appropriate permissions. There is no public registration. This supports both internal team use and selective external collaboration.

**Rationale:** The product needs to extend beyond just the internal team to include external collaborators, but open public registration is not desired. Invite-only access keeps the user base controlled while allowing flexible growth.
