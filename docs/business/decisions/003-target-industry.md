# Decision 003: Target Industry

**Status:** Decided

**Category:** Target Users & Market

**Question:** What industry or domain is TaskFlow targeting?

**Decision:** Industry-agnostic. TaskFlow will not target a specific industry or domain.

**Rationale:** As a demonstration project with an invite-only access model, there is no strong reason to specialize for one industry. Starting industry-agnostic allows general-purpose task management that can specialize later based on actual user feedback. Growth comes from direct invitations rather than industry-targeted marketing.
