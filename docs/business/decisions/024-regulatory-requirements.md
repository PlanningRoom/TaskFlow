# Decision 024: Regulatory Requirements

**Status:** Decided

**Category:** Data & Compliance

**Question:** Are there any regulatory requirements TaskFlow must comply with (e.g., GDPR, SOC 2, HIPAA)?

**Decision:** GDPR-aware with basic privacy best practices. No formal compliance certifications. This includes: not collecting unnecessary personal data, providing account and data deletion, and including a basic privacy policy.

**Rationale:** Even for a demonstration project, basic privacy practices are good hygiene and low effort. The invite-only model means a small, controlled user base, but users may be outside the US, so basic privacy awareness is prudent. Formal certifications are unnecessary for a free demo project.
