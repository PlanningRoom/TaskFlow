# Decision 002: Target Team Size

**Status:** Decided

**Category:** Target Users & Market

**Question:** What team size is TaskFlow designed to support?

**Decision:** Medium teams of 10-50 users.

**Rationale:** The invite-only access model supports project-level collaboration rather than org-wide deployment. Medium team size provides enough structure for roles, permissions, and multiple projects without requiring enterprise-grade complexity. Can expand to larger teams later if demand warrants it.
