# Decision 005: Due Dates

**Status:** Decided

**Category:** Task Features

**Question:** Should tasks have a due date field? If so, how are overdue tasks surfaced — visual indicators on cards, dashboard warnings, or notifications?

**Decision:** Tasks have a due date field (date only, no time). Overdue and approaching-due tasks are surfaced through visual indicators — color changes or icons on task cards and in the dashboard. No due-date-specific notifications at launch.

**Rationale:** The BRD lists due dates as a must-have feature. A simple date field with visual indicators covers the need without expanding the notification system. Date-only keeps it simple — teams think in days, not hours. The due date is interpreted as end of that day in the user's local time zone. Overdue notifications can be layered on later if needed.
