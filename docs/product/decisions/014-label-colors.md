# Decision 014: Label Colors

**Status:** Decided

**Category:** Labels & Tags

**Question:** Do labels have associated colors for visual identification on task cards and filters, or are they text-only?

**Decision:** Labels have colors, selected from a fixed palette when creating or editing a label. Labels display as colored chips (colored background with text) on task cards and in filters.

**Rationale:** Colors provide instant visual identification on the board — users can scan for task categories at a glance without reading label text. A fixed palette (rather than free color picking) ensures colors meet accessibility contrast requirements and look consistent. Colored chips are a familiar pattern used by most task management tools, so users will recognize them immediately.
